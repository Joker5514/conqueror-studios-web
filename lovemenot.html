The Platform

Forensic psychology at your fingertips

Love Me Not brings professional-grade behavioral analysis to individuals navigating complex or potentially harmful relationships. Every analysis is multimodal — the AI looks across text, tone, facial expression, and behavioral patterns simultaneously.

Dark Triad Detection

Identifies narcissistic, Machiavellian, and psychopathic behavioral patterns in communication and interaction history.

Gaslighting Pattern Recognition

Analyzes conversation threads for denial, minimization, and reality-distortion techniques over time.

Coercive Control Analysis

Maps behavioral patterns against established frameworks for identifying coercive and controlling relationship dynamics.

Multimodal Input

Accepts text messages, images, video clips, and audio recordings — combining signals for higher-confidence analysis.

Version 2.0

The system overhaul

Love Me Not v2.0 represents a full architectural rebuild — moving from pattern-matching heuristics to a deep multi-modal AI pipeline capable of cross-referencing behavioral signals across all input types in a single unified analysis pass.

  • Unified Analysis Engine — Single pipeline processes text, audio, and visual signals together.
  • Behavioral Timeline — Tracks escalation patterns and behavioral drift over weeks or months.
  • Evidence-Grade Reporting — Structured output suitable for documentation and professional review.
  • Privacy-First Architecture — All processing is local or encrypted — no behavioral data stored unencrypted.
Analysis Modalities
  • Text — message tone, word choice, frequency patterns.
  • Audio — vocal stress, micro-pauses, emotional register.
  • Video — facial expression, micro-expression detection.
  • Images — context analysis and visual pattern matching.
